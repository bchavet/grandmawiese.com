<pre>
½ cup margarine
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup white sugar

Cream above then add:
1 tsp. vanilla
2 beaten eggs

Sift 2½ cups flour, 1 tsp. soda, ½ tsp. salt
1 lb. orange slices, cut in small pieces
1 cup coconut
1 cup Quick Oatmeal

Chill 4 hours.  Drop on greased cookie sheet.  Bake at 375*
for 10 minutes or less.
</pre>
