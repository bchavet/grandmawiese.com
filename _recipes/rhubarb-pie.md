<pre>
2 c fresh rhubarb, chopped
1 c sugar
t Tbsp flour                     Mix together and pour in unbaked
1 or 2 eggs                      pie shell.  Cover with lattice
                                 or whole pie crust.
Bake at 400° for 15 minutes.
Finish baking at 350° for 45 minutes.
</pre>
