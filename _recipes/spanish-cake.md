<pre>
2 c brown sugar                 1 tsp soda
½ c butter or margarine         1 tsp cinnamon
1 c sour milk                   ½ tsp nutmeg
1 c raisens                     ¼ tsp cloves
2 eggs                          3 c flour

Mix together and pour into 9 x 13 inch pan.  Bake at 325°.
</pre>
